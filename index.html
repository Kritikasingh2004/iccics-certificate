INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON COMPUTATIONAL INTELLIGENCE AND CYBER SECURITY (ICCICS-2023)

Jointly organize by Department of Computer Science & Engineering and the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering Faculty of Engineering and Technology, University of Lucknow, Lucknow

The objective of ICCICS was to hold in-depth discussions on recent advances in computing and communication. Academicians, experts from industry, researchers, and students from all around the world were invited to participate in the conference. The conference also provided a platform for networking and collaboration among universities and institutions for promoting research and creating novel technologies, paving the way for the establishment of a research relationship for the participants.
